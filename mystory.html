Hi and welcome to my webpage!

I'm a father, a husband and soon to be a senior! I spent 25 plus years as a helicopter pilot, working my way up through the ranks. Only to get hit with a medical condition that would ultimately send me on a search for a new purpose. I never thought that I was going to end my career this way, but who really thinks that these things are going to happen to them. Surely, not I!

I was not really prepared for this, but by the grace of God, I began paying for long term disability, 2 years prior to this event. The company paid for short-term disability but long-term was optional. I fortunately paid for long term, even though I remember questioning whether it was worth it or not. Only 8 months prior to this event, during open enrollment I nearly dropped it. When the event occured I frantically checked to see If I had continued it or not. Praise Jesus, I had, I was covered! What a relieve that was, let me tell you!

I started looking for other options and something technology related was the direction I began looking in. I thought that with my talent and years of experience with computers this would be a good place to concentrate my efforts. I quickly learned that I was way behind the power curve. Without a degree and some formal education a career in information technology would be a long shot a best. I alson realized that setting up computers, troubleshooting and repair was not really what I would enjoy doing. In the process of my search I stumbled upon advertisements for coding boot camps. This sparked an interest and I began to investigate just what coding was. Up untill now I didn't realy even know what coding was.
