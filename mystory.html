Hi and welcome to my webpage!

I'm a father, a husband and soon to be a senior! I spent 25 plus years as a helicopter pilot, working my way up through the ranks. Only to get hit with a medical condition that would ultimately send me on a search for a new purpose. I never thought that I was going to end my career this way, but who really thinks that these things are going to happen to them. Surely, not I!

I was not really prepared for this as well, but by the grace of God, I began paying for long term disability, 2 years prior to this event. The company paid for short-term disability but long-term was optional. I fortunately paid for long term, even though I remember questioning whether it was worth it or not. Only 8 months prior to this event, during open enrollment I nearly dropped it. When the event occured I frantically checked to see If I had continued it or not. Praise Jesus, I had, I was covered! What a relieve that was, let me tell you!
